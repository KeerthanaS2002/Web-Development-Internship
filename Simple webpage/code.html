AR/VR Technology

What is AR/VR?

Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) are transformative technologies reshaping how we interact with the digital world. AR overlays digital content onto the real world, enhancing our perception and interaction with our surroundings through devices like smartphones, tablets and AR glasses. This technology is widely used in fields like gaming, education and retail, offering immersive experiences that blend the physical and digital worlds. On the other hand, VR creates entirely virtual environments, fully immersing users in a digital world through headsets and other sensory devices. VR is extensively used in gaming, training simulations and virtual tours, providing users with experiences that are fully detached from the real world. Together, AR and VR are revolutionizing how we experience and interact with both digital and physical worlds, offering limitless possibilities for innovation.
